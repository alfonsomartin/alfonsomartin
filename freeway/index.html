Investigation Summary

I did these sketches on October 29, 2015. From the observations I witnessed a diverse amount of people on my commute. Although, the people that I saw most on the road that day, during that time, was a lot of men that seemed to be Latino/Hispanic. Also, a large amount of commuters I drew seemed to work in construction based on their car and clothing. In doing this project I realized that it is harder to draw people when traffic is moving faster, which is why there are more sketches in the toll plaza area, then there are anywhere else. Again, this is only a general idea of what this commute was like on this day from my perpective. To find out more you can visit the links at the bottom.

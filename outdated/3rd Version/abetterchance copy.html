A BETTER CHANCE

Print and Illustration

A Better Chance's mission is to increase substantially the number of well-educated young people of color who are capable of assuming positions of responsibility and leadership in American society. Since 1963, A Better Chance has been opening the door to educational opportunities for thousands of young people of color in this nation.

THE END OF YEAR CELEBRATION

At the end of the school year A Better Chance holds an event that honors new and current scholors of the program. For the past two years I have helped A Better Chance in designing the program book for their End of Year Celebration.

THE COHORT BINDER

In addition to the EOYC Program Book I also had the opportunity to design the A Better Chance 2018-2019 Cohort Binder. The Cohort Binder is a tool to help cohorts become scholars in the program. It entials the duties and responsiblities of future potential scholars. Before 2018, the Cohort Binder did not recieve much attention in terms of design, but this time A Better Chance wanted to update the Binder to elevate the experience of applying to the program for it's cohorts.

BUBBLE TYPE

Interactive, Type Design

Bubble Type is a typeface made entirely from HTML, CSS, and JQUERY. It is a playful and experimental typeface. For this project, I was very inspired by bubbly pop music and wanted to reflect that into a typeface. I also wanted to take full advantage of it being on screen and push beyond normal static typefaces, so it is interactive when you hover over the bubbles.Check it out live here.

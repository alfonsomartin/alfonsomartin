THE COHORT BINDER

The Cohort Binder is a tool to help cohorts become scholars in the program. It contains the duties and responsiblities for a cohort to become a full scholar in the program. Before 2018, the Cohort Binder did not recieve much attention in terms of design, but this coming school year A Better Chance wanted to elevate the experience of applying for it's cohorts.

Bubble Type is a playful and experimental typeface, that was designed by Alfonso Martin in Fall of 2015 for Interactive 2, taught by Laurel Schwulst. Bubble Type is an interactive typeface that is made up from bubbles of various random sizes. Each time you refresh the page a different outcome and form of letters will appear.

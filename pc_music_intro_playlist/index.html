PC Music Intro Playlist

PC Music is a record label and art collective based in London and run by producer A. G. Cook. This a playlist of songs from each member and some songs from artists associated to the label.

Beautiful - A. G. Cook
Bubblegum rave party!

1UL - Danny L Harle
I would consider him as EDM.

X-RAY - Tommy Cash (produced by Danny L Harle)
This song goes hard.

I.D.L. - Life Sim
Basically is just one long build up. Has an interesting music video. I don't know what IDL stands for.

Be Your USA (feat. Iiris) - EASYFUN
Sick lyric video

Tongue - GFOTY
Her songs are all over the place. She recently left the label to go independant.

Distant Promise - Spinee
She does these sick DJ sets on NTS radio once every month. Mostly Hardstyle.

Track 10 - Charli XCX
Produced by a couple PC members. Listen all the way, it might be a lot, but i think it's amazing.

Hey QT - QT
Perfromance artist, CEO of a fictional energy drink, very Meta. They also have an interesting short film.

Chemistry - umru, Lewis Grant
umru newest member, I got a picture with him at the STUD, he DJ'd there earlier this year. It was awesome!

Every Night - Hannah Diamond
Hyper Bubblegum Pop Princess.

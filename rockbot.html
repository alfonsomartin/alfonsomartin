ROCKBOT

Print, Digital, Installation

Rockbot is a music platform changing the way businesses everywhere control their soundtracks and engage their guests. During my time at Rockbot, I designed and managed several projects including: packaging, web design, graphic illustrations for print and digital, icons, guides and installations.

THE SHIPPING BOX

For the full Rockbot experience customers would recieve a Media Player. This player would be shipped in a standard brown shipping box. We decided that the shipping box could use some branding to help elevate the customer experience. In addition to the box, I also designed the printed inserts that went along with the Media Player.

INSTALLATIONS

To help excite and inspire the team, I was given the opportunity to design an installation within the Rockbot headquarters. The visual inspiration for the wall mural came from restaurant dining walls. This felt appropriate for the installation since a large percent of venues that used Rockbot were restaurants. The wall became filled with information pertaining to the companies relationship with its clients.

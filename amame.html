AMAME

Identity, Branding, Logo, Styling

Amame is a Latinx club and bar that caters towards the Latinx LGBTQA community. The idea for this club and bar was influenced by the tragic Pulse Night Club shooting back in June 2016. I wanted to make my own night club that was specifically for the Latinx community as a way of coping and making something meaningful from a horrible situation.

WHY THE NAME AMAME?

Amame means "love me" in Spanish. It is also a hit song by iconic Mexican-American singer Selena Quintanilla-Pérez. As many know, during the peak of her career Selena tragically passed away from a gun shot at the young age of 23. Her music has forever been admired by the Latinx community and I felt that the parallel between the two events held some significance, which is why I thought Amame was an appropriate name for this project.

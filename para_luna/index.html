PC Music Intro Para Luna

PC Music is a record label and art collective based in London and run by producer A. G. Cook. These are some songs I thought you might like from some of the artists associated with the label :)


Beautiful - A. G. Cook
Bubblegum rave party!


1UL - Danny L Harle
Does mostly EDM-ish kind of music.


X-RAY - Tommy Cash (produced by Danny L Harle)
This song is such a banger!


I.D.L. - Life Sim
This song is basically one long build up. Love the music video for this one. It's just clips of cinematic moments from hella different movie trailers put in one video to form this like universal narrative.


Be Your USA (feat. Iiris) - EASYFUN
Nothing much to say. I just think it's a good song haha


Tongue - GFOTY
Love GFOTY. Her songs are so absurd, "I like it when we kiss with the tongue, muscle of fun inside my mouth" like what? lol (she actually just left the label this year to go independant)


Track 10 - Charli XCX
Couple of PC peeps worked on production for this one. It's so good. Beginning sounds crazy, but listen all the way through it builds up and gets crazier.


Hey QT - QT
Perfromance artist that pretends to be pop star/CEO of a fictional energy drink, very Meta, haha. This is their one song ever and they also have an interesting short film too.


Chemistry - umru, Lewis Grant
umru is a new member, I got a picture with him at the STUD, he DJ'd there earlier this year. It was a great set.


Distant Promise - Spinee
She does these sick DJ sets on NTS radio once every month. More Hardstyle kind of music.


Fade Away - Hannah Diamond
She is awesome. Don't think you'll like her lol, but she has a special place in my heart lol so had to include her.

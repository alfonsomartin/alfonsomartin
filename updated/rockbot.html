ROCKBOT

Print, Digital, Installation

Rockbot is a music platform for different businesses and industries. With Rockbot clients are able to control and curate the perfect type of music for their specific needs. In addition to this service, Rockbot also has an app, in which users can engage with the music at certain locations.

As a Rockbot Intern, and later full-time Junior Graphic Designer, I made graphics for digital, print and marketing purposes. The time I was there I developed a new shipping box and printed assets to go along with the box, layed out some web pages within the Rockbot website, adapted the Rockbot logo for the Rockbot Remote App and created two seperate installations within the Rockbot Headquarters in Oakland.

THE ROCKBOT SHIPPING BOX

Whenever a client decided they wanted the full Rockbot experience, they would be sent a Rockbot Media Player. The player would be shipped in a standard brown cardboard box. It was brought to our attention that sometimes business owners would lose or not realize they recieved the player. It was also decided that it was time Rockbot added their branding to the box. For these reasons I was assigned to make graphics for the shipping box.

I also made printed inserts to go along with the Media Player. This included an instructions guide for installing the Media Player and a sticker sheet for cable organization.

THE ROCKBOT INSTALLATIONS

I was also given the task to create some sort of installation within the Rockbot kitchen that showcased the companies relationship with the clients. The visual inspiration for the installation came from restaurant dining walls, since a large percent of Rockbot customers were restaurants, and also it was in the kitchen. The wall became filled with information pertaining to customer success.

The second installation was a bit more self-motivated. At the time I had access to a laser cutter, so I took the opportunity to fill in this empty decrepit part of the wall in the office with a custom lasercut Rockbot sign.

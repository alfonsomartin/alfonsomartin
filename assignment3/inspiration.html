Making Masks 101

Masks are always fun to make and wear. They are really simple to make and there are a million ways to make them. In this How-To website, I am going to show you how to make a plaster wrap mask for Day of the Dead. Now, you are probably asking yourself, "Why do I want to make a Day of the Dead Mask?" The real question you should be asking yourself is why don't I already have a mask for Day of the Dead? Well, Day of the Dead is a great tradition in Latin America and it is becoming very popular and trendy all over, especially in the past few years. People love the Day of the Dead aeusthetic and who can blame them its a fun and lovely aeusthetic. So get ready to get your hands dirty because this project can get messy but it will be worth it!
